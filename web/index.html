Dynamics Validation Metrics

  • Reconstruction MSE: 0.000640
    During the first 10 steps (Context phase), the model uses real pixel observations to form posterior distributions, giving high-fidelity reconstructions.
  • Prediction MSE: 0.000670
    In the open-loop dreaming phase (steps 10 to 40), the model predicts future visual dynamics entirely using transition priors. The error remains low, showing high recurrent stability.
  • Reward Prediction
    The reward predictor accurately maps the latent states $(h_t, s_t)$ to environmental rewards, capturing the peaks and valleys of running speed even when dreaming.
